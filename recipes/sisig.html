Sisig

Description

Pork sisig is a popular Filipino dish. It can be considered as a main dish or
an appetizer. The composition of this dish is mainly of pig’s parts such as
minced pork meat, ears, and face. Chicken liver, onions, and chili peppers
are also added.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams pig's masrk or pork belly, boiled in water with black
    pepperocrns, bay leaves, onion and garlic until tender, drained
  • Fish sauce
  • Black pepper
  • Canola oil
  • 1 pc red onion, chopped
  • 1-inch pc ginder, chopped
  • 1/4 cup chicken liver, chopped
  • 1 pc green chili, sliced
  • Bird's eye chilli, sliced (as needed)
  • Knorr Liquid Seasoning (as needed)
  • 5 tbsps Mayonnaise
  • 3 pcs calamansi

Steps

  1. Season boiled meats then grill or cook in a 375F oven until crispy.
    Chop into small cubes.
  2. In pan, sauté onion and ginger. Add chicken liver and sauté until
    cooked. Add the chopped meats, and chilies. Season with Knorr
    Liquid Seasoning according to taste.
  3. Off the heat, add Lady’s Choice Real Mayonnaise and mix well.
    Serve hot with calamansi.
